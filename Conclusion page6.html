Conclusion

Oct 29, 2020


Developments in e-learning and technologies are creating the groundwork for a revolution in education, allowing learning to be individualized, enhancing learners' interactions with each other, and transforming the role of the teacher. In spite of the fact that e-learning has quickly developed in the field of instruction, more endeavors are expected to make it accessible to everybody.
E-learning changes the way students think and study and can help students to grow with a better aptitude. In very broad terms then, there are two groups to be appear using lecture recordings: one, using them as a substitute for lectures, the other, as a compliment.
